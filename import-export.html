Our Import Export Process

A structured, transparent workflow ensuring seamless international trade from consultation to delivery

1

meeting_room Initial Consultation

We discuss your product requirements, conduct market analysis, review quality standards, and provide detailed cost estimates. Required documents include product specifications, technical datasheets, and business registration.

2

verified Certification & Compliance

We identify and verify required certifications (ISO, CE, FDA), ensure regulatory compliance, and handle licensing requirements. Documents include existing certificates, test reports, and compliance checklists.

3

handshake Pricing & Terms

Negotiate pricing, payment terms (LC, TT), shipping terms (FOB, CIF, EXW), and Incoterms agreement. We prepare proforma invoice, price quotation, and finalize packaging specifications.

4

description Contract Finalization

Draft and review sales contract, finalize terms, obtain signatures, and confirm payment arrangements. Key documents include purchase contract, purchase order (PO), and Letter of Credit.

5

inventory Procurement & Production

Coordinate with suppliers, monitor production progress, conduct quality inspections, and arrange proper packaging. Regular production status reports and quality certificates provided.

6

assignment Documentation & Logistics

Prepare complete trade documentation including commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, and bill of lading. Arrange shipping and book cargo with reliable carriers.

7

verified_user Customs Clearance

Submit customs declarations with proper HS code classification, coordinate clearance procedures, handle duty and tax payments, and provide real-time tracking throughout transit.

8

delivery_dining Delivery & Support

Confirm successful delivery, obtain proof of delivery (POD), handle any post-delivery issues, and provide ongoing support. Complete transaction with final invoice and summary.

* Please note that the processes listed above are not mandatory. They can vary from one case to another based on individual circumstances.
